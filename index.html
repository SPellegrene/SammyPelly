THE 80/20 RULE

Many say the customer is always right. I would disagree and say that most of the time, consumers do not know what they want. It is the job of a brand to aid in these desires. While working in the music industry and at Arrow, I learned the 80/20 rule. Give the customer 80% of something they recognize and 20% of something brand new. I have tried to practice this rule within my own music, as well as in my development practices.
